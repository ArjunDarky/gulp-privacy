Privacy Policy

Gulp - Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gulp ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the mobile game. Gulp is a casual 3D black hole city-eater game.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The App may store gameplay information on your device, such as:

  • Game progress, selected level, stars, best scores, and total score.
  • Audio mute settings.
  • Ad-related local settings, such as whether ads have been removed or how many plays have occurred.

This information is stored locally on your device and is used to make the game work properly.

Advertising

The App may show ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, crash data, diagnostics, and other data described by Google's policies. This information may be used to show ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve ad services.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

You can manage ad personalization through your device settings or through Google's ad settings: https://adssettings.google.com/.

Internet Permission

The App uses internet access to support services such as advertising and normal app functionality. We do not use this permission to directly collect personal contact information from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by third-party service providers, such as Google AdMob, as described in their own privacy policies. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the security of the App.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless you clear the App's data or uninstall the App. Advertising data collected by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies.

Your Choices

  • You can clear local game data from your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove locally stored gameplay data from your device.
  • You can manage ad personalization in your device or Google account settings.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the App and the information it handles. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
